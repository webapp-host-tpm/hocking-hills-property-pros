Questions

How this stays useful and fair.

Do featured partners pay for placement?

Some providers may be Featured Local Partners and receive enhanced placement, video profiles, case studies, and promotional visibility. The matching process should still prioritize project fit.

Do you sell my lead to multiple contractors?

The goal is not to blast a project to a dozen companies. Simple projects may receive one best-fit recommendation and a backup. Larger projects may receive a small lineup of relevant trades.

Are you acting as the general contractor?

No. Hocking Hills Property Pros provides information, directory listings, and match recommendations. Property owners choose, verify, hire, and contract directly with service providers.

Can contractors join for free?

Yes. A basic listing can be free to help build a complete local index. Pro and Featured profiles provide better presentation, more visibility, and promotional options.
